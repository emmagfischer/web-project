Queens Street Jazz

For this project I decided to capture a glimpse of the vibrant sounds I encounter daily in New York City. Every trip on the 7 train from my home in Jackson Heights, Queens into downtown Manhattan, I pass through a multitude of languages, cultures, and music. This project is a small glimpse into the auditory magic of Queens and an ode to my love for jazz. To construct this soundscape I layered and mixed 6 different sounds recorded on my phone over the past 2 years. I mostly played with fading in and out of each sound to create a transitory effect.

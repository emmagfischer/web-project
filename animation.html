Yugen

For this project I chose the Japanese emotion, yugen, meaning the ache of noticing how beautiful something is while it's still here — “yu” meaning dim or difficult to see and “gen” meaning the dark, tranquil color of the universe. In my animation I chose to represent a shadow of a bare tree cast mid-afternoon after a snow storm, a moment that would only last until the sunlight slipped both the shadow and snow into alternate states. I used this image to create a vector shape of the cast shadow. For the background I used an image of the stars I took at Soul Fire Farm in October— it was another moment I felt yugen, a fleeting feeling of beauty and awareness of the universe around me.

_____Made in Adobe After Affects and Illustrator_____
